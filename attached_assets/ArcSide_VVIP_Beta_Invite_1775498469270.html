📋 HOW TO TEST THE APP

Step 1: Access the App

Visit app.arcside.co.za and log in with your whitelisted email. Your access is secured by our "Bouncer" protocol—only verified professionals can enter.

Step 2: Core Features to Test

🔧 Parameter Engine
Input material type, thickness, and welding process (MIG/TIG/Stick). Verify that calculated voltage and amperage settings are accurate for your typical jobs. Test edge cases (thin materials, thick materials, different processes).
📝 Digital Job Log
Create a new job entry. Record weld specifications, attach a photo from your workshop, and save settings. Verify that data persists and can be retrieved later. Test on both desktop and mobile.
🤖 AI Defect Analyzer
Upload or take a photo of a weld (good or defective). Check if the AI correctly identifies defects and provides actionable suggestions. Test with multiple weld types.
📄 WPS Generator
Generate a Welding Procedure Specification (WPS) document. Verify that it's ISO/AWS compliant and ready for professional use. Test export functionality (PDF, Word).

Step 3: Test on Your Device

  • Mobile First: Test primarily on your smartphone (iOS or Android). This is a field tool.
  • Real Conditions: Use the app in your workshop or on-site. Test performance under poor lighting, network interruptions, and real workload.
  • Cross-Device: If possible, test on tablet and desktop to ensure consistency.

Step 4: Look for Issues

  • Does the app crash or freeze?
  • Are calculations accurate compared to your manual methods?
  • Is the UI intuitive or confusing?
  • Does it work offline? Does it sync when reconnected?
  • Are there missing features that would make your job easier?

💬 HOW TO GIVE CONSTRUCTIVE FEEDBACK

What We're Looking For

  • Bugs & Crashes: Exact steps to reproduce, device info, error messages.
  • Accuracy Issues: If calculations seem wrong, tell us your material, thickness, process, and expected vs. actual results.
  • UX Feedback: Is something confusing? Suggest how to improve it.
  • Feature Requests: What would make this tool invaluable for your workflow?
  • Performance: Is it fast enough? Does it drain battery? Does it work on slow networks?

Feedback Format

Use the form below. Include as much detail as possible:

  • Category: Bug, Feature Request, UX Feedback, Accuracy Issue, Performance
  • Title: Brief summary of the issue
  • Description: Detailed explanation with steps to reproduce (if applicable)
  • Device Info: Phone model, OS, app version (if shown)
  • Severity: Critical (blocks work), High (major issue), Medium (minor issue), Low (nice to have)
